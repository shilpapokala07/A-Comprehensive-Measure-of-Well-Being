Human Development Index (HDI)

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical measure developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to evaluate the overall development of a country. It considers three major dimensions:

  • Life Expectancy
  • Education (Schooling)
  • Gross National Income (GNI) per Capita

HDI helps compare countries based on health, education, and income rather than economic growth alone. This web application uses a Machine Learning model to predict the Human Development Index based on user-provided values.
